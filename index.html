Ten little Soldier Boys went out to dine; One choked his little elf and then there were nine.
Nine little Soldier Boys sat up very lae; One overslept himself and then there were eight.
Eight little Soldier Boys traveling in Devon; One said he'd stay thee and then there were seven.
Seven little Soldier Boys chopping up sticks; One chopped himself in hlves and then there were six.
Six little Soldier Boys playing with a hive; A bumblebee sung one and then there were five.
Five little Soldier Boys going in for law; One got in Chancry and then there were four.
Four little Soldier Boys oing out to sea; A red herring swallowed one and then there were three.
Three little Soldier Boys walking in the zoo; A bg bear hugged one and then there were two.
Two little Soldier Boys sitting in the sun; One got frizzld up and then there was one.
One little Soldier Boy left all alone; He went and hanged himelf and then there were none.
